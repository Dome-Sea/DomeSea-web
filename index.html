What is DomeSea?

DomeSea will become one of the premier destinations in the world for scuba divers and family's looking for an indoor adventure unlike any other. As an all-in-one theme park, DomeSea will offer guests the opportunity to experience the world under water, the thrill of riding some of the top waterslides, channeling their inner child with a barrage of games, or in achieving the dreams of becoming an Olympic swimmer in the state-of-the-art aquatic center.

Photos here

With four adventure parks rolled up into one indoor facility, DomeSea's approach is to provide entertainment and adventure to all ages and all abilities. The central feature of this venture will be the largest indoor dive park in the US where guests can enjoy the tranquility of the sea. The best diving locations around the world will be reproduced in our 8 pools that range from 90 to 150 feet in diameter and depths up to 100 feet. Imagine being able to swim in Fiji, the Caribbean, and the Catalina Islands all in one day. Guests will be able to explore a ship wreck, practice skills, gain certifications, and more in a tropical-like environment.
