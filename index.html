What is Dome Sea?

What do you get when you combine a waterpark, a fun center, hotels, and restaurants with scuba diving? You get the ultimate family fun adventure, that only Dome Sea has to offer! With the largest indoor waterpark and dive pools, Dome Sea is your one stop shop for your next adventure. Whether you are coming for the day, a weekend, a week, or even longer! Dome Sea has all the things you expect a resort. Fun, food, and relaxation all in one. Not SCUBA Certified. No problem! Our team of PADI Dive Instructures are here to help! Need dive equipment? We have the latest and greatest in our onsite dive shop, ran by North American Divers.
