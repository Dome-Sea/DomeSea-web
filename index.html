What is Dome Sea?

Dome Sea will be Utah's premier indoor family adventure. Dome Sea will host the world's largest indoor scuba pool, an indoor waterpark with extreme rides, wave pool, lazy river, and several splash pads, an arcade and entertainment center with a virtual reality center, laser tag, mini golf, rope course, and play center, restaurants, and a mid-level to luxury hotel.
